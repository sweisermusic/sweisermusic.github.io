Biography

Sam Weiser, 20, is currently a junior in the dual-degree program with Tufts University and the New England Conservatory in Boston, MA. He is pursuing a degree in computer science from Tufts and in violin performance at NEC, where his current teacher is James Buswell. Sam has worked under the tutelage of remarkable musicians, such as Patinka Kopec, Daniel Heifetz, and Mark O'Connor. He is also a former recipient of the Daniel Pearl Memorial Violin. Sam has been the concertmaster of the Tanglewood Music Center Orchestra, and has done no notable computer science work so far in his career.
